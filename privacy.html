MyIP

Privacy Policy

Overview

Last updated: March 8, 2026

MyIP is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

What the App Does

MyIP retrieves your public IP address and approximate location, including city, region, and country, by sending a request to third-party IP lookup services. It also reads your device's local WiFi IP address.

This information is displayed on-screen and is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any server operated by us.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services to look up your public IP address and approximate location:

ipinfo.io
https://ipinfo.io/privacy
ip-api.com
https://ip-api.com/docs/legal

These services receive your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash reports

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Clipboard

The app copies your IP address to the clipboard only when you explicitly choose to do so via the context menu. No data is copied automatically.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
